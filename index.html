FAQ

How do I earn SPADE Points?

You earn SPADE Points by participating in key SPADE activities, including incidents, inquiries, PVQs, training courses, and risk mitigation projects. Points are accumulated each quarter based on your participation.

How do I redeem gifts with SPADE Points?

Navigate to the Non-Monetary section, enter your SPADE Points, and view available gifts. Select the gift you want to redeem and follow the instructions to complete the redemption process.

How are monetary rewards determined?

Monetary rewards are based on the impact level of your contribution. High-impact contributions such as critical incidents or complex inquiries are eligible for higher cash rewards through the nomination process.

How often are points updated?

Points are updated quarterly based on your participation in SPADE activities during that period. The OPM team collects data from various sources and updates the points accordingly.
