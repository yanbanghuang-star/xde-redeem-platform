Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between PC and BP?

PC (Progress Credit) is for learning and proves your accuracy. BP (Bonus Points) is for doing and rewards your consistency. You need both for the best rewards.

What can I get with just PC?

100 PC unlocks a standard tier of rewards and, more importantly, gives you the ability to spend any BP you earn.

Where can I see my points?

You can track your PC and BP balance in your personal SPADE dashboard. View Dashboard

Do points expire?

We aim for flexibility. Currently, points do not expire, but we encourage timely redemption as reward collections may change.
